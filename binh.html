Task 2 · Discuss both views and give your opinion
Q
Exam Task
Task Prompt Scientists agree that many people eat too much junk food and it is damaging their health. Some people think that this problem can be solved by education, while others believe that this education will not work. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
✍
Candidate's Essay
Written by Bình

Some individuals believe that education can help people not overeat junk food to protect their health, while others suggest that this education will not work efficiently. In my opinion, education can tackle this issue in some cases, because it completely depends on the choices of people receiving that education.

On the one hand, education plays an important role in preventing the increase of junk food consumption. Firstly, it is good for children to receive education about these issues when they are in school. When children are taught about the drawbacks of overeating junk food, they can gain deep understanding to know what is good for their health. For example, lessons in schools including knowledge about the properties of junk food or the negative side of eating too much that kind of food can raise awareness of children. As a result, children know the proper amount of junk food they can take in. Secondly, governments should organize some awareness campaigns for adults to raise their awareness. As some adults are too busy at 9-5 work, they would like to eat junk food to save time on cooking or if they have kids, they will allow their children to eat junk food instead of preparing home made dishes for their kids. Therefore, when there are some awareness campaigns about overeating junk food and spending more time on cooking will enhance their mental health, adults can expand their knowledge and pay more attention to their diet. After that, they can affect their children by teaching and cooking foods for their kids.

On the other hand, receiving proper education is a part of addressing this problem, because it depends on the choices. Gaining enough knowledge about the consequences of eating too much junk food does not mean that they can control their consumption of that food. Because, there are no rules about eating junk food, so they can eat that food freely as they want. In some cases when people feel stressed, they will tend to overeat junk food. For these reasons, education is just a tool helping them to be aware about their choice of eating junk food, people will be the final person to determine whether to eat or not.

Đoạn này chưa được phát triển đầy đủ. Phía ủng hộ giáo dục đưa ra hai luận điểm rõ ràng, có ví dụ minh họa, trong khi phía phản biện gần như chỉ xoay quanh một ý. Nên bổ sung một lý do riêng biệt thứ hai để giải thích vì sao giáo dục không hiệu quả.

(This paragraph is under-developed. The pro-education side gets two fully developed points with examples, while the counter-argument side gets essentially one idea restated multiple times. You should add a second distinct reason why education fails.)

In conclusion, education is needed to help people enhance their awareness about the consequences of eating too much junk food. However, people still hold the power on choosing what they want to eat and how much they want to take in that kind of food.

Scores & Feedback by Criterion
Task Response
6.5

The essay addresses both views and presents a personal opinion. The position — that education helps but ultimately depends on individual choice — is original and coherent. Both body paragraphs contain relevant ideas that are generally extended.

However, there are weaknesses preventing Band 7: the "against education" argument is underdeveloped compared to the "for education" side, which gets two well-elaborated points while the opposing view relies mainly on one central idea (personal choice) repeated in different ways. The opinion is also somewhat hedged and cautious rather than clearly defended. Read Huy's essay, which scored 7 in Task Response, to see how to develop better ideas for this task.

Coherence & Cohesion
6.5

The essay is generally well-organised with a clear overall progression. Paragraph structure is logical and the writer uses a good range of cohesive devices: firstly, secondly, as a result, therefore, however, after that, for these reasons.

However, some issues limit the score. "After that" in the first body paragraph is an odd connector — it implies a temporal sequence where a logical consequence is intended (as a result or in turn would be better). The second body paragraph opens with "receiving proper education is a part of addressing this problem" — this reads more like agreement than a counterargument, which blurs the paragraph's purpose. The sentence beginning "Because, there are no rules..." is a fragment, not a full sentence.

Lexical Resource
6.5

There is a reasonable range of vocabulary used with generally appropriate precision: drawbacks, overeating, raise awareness, consequences, enhance their mental health, awareness campaigns. The writer attempts some less common phrasing and largely succeeds.

However, repetition is a notable weakness — junk food appears roughly fifteen times without meaningful variation, and awareness is overused across both body paragraphs. Some word choices are imprecise or unnatural: "take in" (consume would be more formal and appropriate), "the final person to determine" (the one who ultimately decides), "that kind of food" appears repeatedly as an awkward substitute for a better synonym. Spelling appears clean throughout, which is a strength.

Grammatical Range & Accuracy
6.5

The essay demonstrates a mix of simple and complex structures, with complex sentences generally handled well. There is evidence of subordinate clauses, conditionals, and relative clauses used with reasonable accuracy.

Errors exist but are mostly minor and do not impede communication: "know what is good for their health" is slightly redundant following "deep understanding," "spending more time on cooking will enhance their mental health" is a debatable and grammatically awkward claim, "people will be the final person" shows number disagreement (people...person). The fragment sentence ("Because, there are no rules...") is the most notable grammatical lapse. Overall, error-free sentences are frequent enough to suggest solid grammatical control at this level.

Task Response (Đáp ứng đề)
6.5

Bài luận có đề cập đến cả hai quan điểm và có đưa ra ý kiến cá nhân. Lập trường của người viết (giáo dục có thể giúp nhưng cuối cùng vẫn phụ thuộc vào lựa chọn của mỗi cá nhân) rất hay và hợp lý. Cả hai đoạn thân bài đều có ý liên quan và được phát triển ở mức nhất định.

Tuy nhiên, vẫn còn những điểm hạn chế khiến bài chưa đạt Band 7: lập luận "giáo dục không hiệu quả" còn yếu hơn so với phía "giáo dục có hiệu quả". Phía ủng hộ giáo dục có hai ý được triển khai khá đầy đủ, trong khi phía phản đối chủ yếu dựa vào một ý chính (lựa chọn cá nhân) và lặp lại theo nhiều cách khác nhau. Nên đọc bài của Huy (đạt Band 7 ở tiêu chí Task Response) để tham khảo cách phát triển ý tốt hơn cho bài này.

Coherence & Cohesion (Liên kết và mạch lạc)
6.5

Bài viết nhìn chung được tổ chức tốt với mạch phát triển rõ ràng. Cấu trúc đoạn hợp lý và người viết sử dụng khá đa dạng các từ nối như firstly, secondly, as a result, therefore, however, after that, for these reasons.

Tuy nhiên, vẫn có một số vấn đề làm hạn chế điểm. Cụm "after that" trong đoạn thân bài đầu tiên là không phù hợp. "After that" dùng cho trình tự thời gian, trong khi ở đây cần thể hiện quan hệ logic (as a result hoặc in turn sẽ phù hợp hơn). Đoạn thân bài thứ hai mở đầu bằng "receiving proper education is a part of addressing this problem" nghe giống như đang đồng ý hơn là phản biện, làm mục đích của đoạn trở nên không rõ ràng. Ngoài ra, câu bắt đầu bằng "Because, there are no rules..." là một mệnh đề chưa hoàn chỉnh, không phải là một câu đầy đủ.

Lexical Resource (Từ vựng và diễn đạt)
6.5

Có vốn từ vựng khá tốt và nhìn chung được sử dụng tương đối chính xác: drawbacks, overeating, raise awareness, consequences, enhance their mental health, awareness campaigns. Người viết có cố gắng dùng các cách diễn đạt ít phổ biến hơn và phần lớn là thành công.

Tuy nhiên, lặp từ là một điểm yếu quan trọng. Junk food xuất hiện nhiều lần mà không có từ thay thế, awareness cũng bị dùng lặp lại ở cả hai đoạn thân bài. Một số cách dùng từ chưa tự nhiên hoặc chính xác: "take in" (nên dùng "consume" trong văn phong học thuật), "the final person to determine" (nên là "the one who ultimately decides"), và "that kind of food" bị lặp lại như một cách thay thế chưa hiệu quả. Điểm mạnh là không có lỗi chính tả đáng kể.

Grammatical Range & Accuracy (Ngữ pháp)
6.5

Bài viết sử dụng kết hợp cả câu đơn và câu phức, trong đó các cấu trúc phức nhìn chung được xử lý khá tốt. Có bằng chứng về việc sử dụng mệnh đề phụ, câu điều kiện và mệnh đề quan hệ với độ chính xác tương đối.

Vẫn có một số lỗi, nhưng chủ yếu là nhỏ và không ảnh hưởng đến việc hiểu: "know what is good for their health" hơi dư ý sau "deep understanding", "spending more time on cooking will enhance their mental health" vừa gây tranh cãi về nội dung vừa chưa tự nhiên về mặt ngữ pháp, "people will be the final person" bị lỗi số ít/số nhiều (people…person). Câu rời "Because, there are no rules..." là lỗi ngữ pháp đáng chú ý nhất. Nhìn chung, số lượng câu đúng hoàn toàn đủ nhiều để cho thấy khả năng kiểm soát ngữ pháp khá vững ở mức này.

What Would Push This to Band 7
  • 1

    Balance the two sides more equally.

    The pro-education side gets two fully developed points with examples; the counter-argument side gets essentially one idea restated multiple times. A second distinct reason why education fails, such as the influence of food marketing, price and convenience barriers, or psychological factors like addiction, would significantly strengthen Task Response.

  • 2

    Reduce repetition of key terms.

    Replacing repeated instances of junk food with processed food, fast food, unhealthy food options and replacing repeated awareness with understanding, consciousness, knowledge would lift the Lexical Resource score noticeably.

  • 3

    Fix the sentence fragment.

    "Because, there are no rules..." must be joined to the preceding sentence. In academic writing, because should not begin a standalone sentence.

  • 4

    Sharpen the personal opinion.

    The position is interesting but somewhat passive — the essay tells us education depends on choices without arguing forcefully for what should therefore be done. A clearer stance, even one sentence defending a specific recommendation, would make the opinion more convincing.
